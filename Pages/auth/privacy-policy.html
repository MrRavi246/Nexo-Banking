Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 10, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Nexo collects various types of information to provide you with secure banking services:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, date of birth, and address provided during registration
  • Financial Information: Account numbers, transaction history, and payment details
  • Usage Data: Analytics and behavioral data to improve our services and user experience
  • Technical Data: IP addresses, browser type, device information, and cookies for security and authentication
  • Communication Data: Records of your interactions with our customer support team

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively for legitimate banking purposes:

  • Provide and maintain secure banking services and account management
  • Process transactions, transfers, and bill payments
  • Communicate with you about your account, transactions, and service updates
  • Enhance security measures and prevent fraud
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • Improve our services through analytics and user feedback
  • Provide customer support and resolve issues

3. Data Protection & Security

We implement industry-leading security measures to protect your sensitive information:

  • 256-bit SSL encryption for all data transmission
  • Advanced fraud detection and monitoring systems
  • Multi-factor authentication and biometric security options
  • Regular security audits and compliance checks
  • Secure data centers with 24/7 monitoring
  • Employee training on data protection and privacy

4. Information Sharing

Nexo does not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations and regulatory requirements
  • With trusted service providers who assist in our operations (under strict confidentiality agreements)
  • To prevent fraud or protect the security of our services
  • In case of business transfer or merger (with prior notification)

5. Your Privacy Rights

You have several rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Update: Correct or update your personal information at any time
  • Delete: Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal requirements)
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data to another service provider
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications
  • Restrict Processing: Limit how we process your information in certain circumstances

6. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience and ensure security:

  • Essential cookies for authentication and security
  • Performance cookies to analyze and improve our services
  • Functional cookies to remember your preferences
  • You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings

7. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy and as required by law. Financial records may be retained for longer periods to comply with regulatory requirements.

8. International Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information during such transfers.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any material changes via email or through our platform.

10. Contact Information
